2. Cherry Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen cherries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup yogurt or almond milk
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Serve chilled and enjoy as a refreshing drink!

3. Chocolate-Covered Cherries

Ingredients:

  • 20 fresh cherries (with stems)
  • 200g dark chocolate

Instructions: Melt chocolate in a bowl. Dip each cherry halfway, place on baking paper, and let cool until chocolate hardens.
