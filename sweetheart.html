The Sweetheart Cherry

Originating from the gentle valleys of British Columbia, the Sweetheart cherry is cherished for its late-season harvest and irresistible sweetness. Its deep red, heart-shaped fruit is a symbol of affection and joy, making it a favorite for celebrations and special moments.

Whether enjoyed fresh, in desserts, or as a gift, Sweetheart cherries are a testament to nature's artistry and the bonds we share with loved ones.
