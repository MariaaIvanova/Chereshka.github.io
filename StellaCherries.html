Tree Characteristics

Vigorous, spreading growth habit reaching 15-20 feet tall and wide. Produces heavy crops and is excellent for both commercial orchards and home gardens.

Climate Requirements

Thrives in USDA zones 5-8, requiring 700-800 chill hours. Adapts well to various climates and is more cold-hardy than many other sweet cherry varieties.

Care & Maintenance

Low maintenance variety that responds well to annual pruning. Regular watering during fruit development ensures optimal size and quality. Resistant to many common cherry diseases.

Stella Cherry Characteristics

Fruit Size
Medium to large cherries, typically 0.8-1.2 inches in diameter with good uniformity and attractive appearance.
Color & Appearance
Deep red to almost black skin when fully ripe. Firm, dark red flesh with excellent color retention.
Flavor Profile
Sweet with rich cherry flavor and subtle tartness. Excellent balance that appeals to most palates.
Texture
Firm, crisp flesh that holds up well to handling and storage. Good resistance to splitting and bruising.
Harvest Time
Mid-season variety, typically ripening in early to mid-summer. Harvest window of 7-10 days when fruits are at peak ripeness.
Storage
Good storage qualities, can be refrigerated for 1-2 weeks. Maintains firmness and flavor when properly stored.
