Popular Cherry Varieties

Bing Cherries
Large, dark red cherries with sweet, juicy flesh. Perfect for fresh eating and desserts.
Rainier Cherries
Golden-yellow with a red blush. Exceptionally sweet with a delicate, creamy texture.
Montmorency
Bright red tart cherries. Ideal for pies, preserves, and juice production.
Stella Cherries
Self-pollinating sweet cherries with dark red skin and firm, sweet flesh.
Sweetheart Cherries
Late-season cherries with heart-shaped fruit, deep red color, and exceptional sweetness.
Lapins Cherries
Large, dark red cherries with excellent flavor. Self-fertile and disease-resistant variety.
