Tart, Bright and Bursting with Flavor

Montmorency is America’s favorite tart cherry, famous for its bold, tangy taste and ruby‑red hue—perfect for pies, preserves, and healthful snacking.

About Montmorency

Named after France’s Montmorency Valley, these cherries thrive in cooler climates and are now grown mainly in Michigan.

Flavor & Use

Distinctly tart, making them ideal for baking, canning, and juicing—often sold dried or frozen.

Quick Facts

  • 🥧 The classic pie cherry
  • 🧃 High in antioxidants & melatonin
  • ❄️ Widely available dried or frozen year‑round
  • 🇺🇸 90 % of U.S. crop comes from Michigan
