Lapins Cherry Characteristics

Fruit Size
Large to very large cherries, typically 1-1.5 inches in diameter with excellent uniformity.
Color & Appearance
Deep red to mahogany skin with firm, light red flesh. Attractive glossy finish when ripe.
Flavor Profile
Sweet with a subtle tartness, rich cherry flavor with excellent balance and complexity.
Texture
Firm, crisp flesh that holds up well to handling and transportation without bruising.
Harvest Time
Mid to late season variety, typically ripening 2-3 weeks after Bing cherries in most regions.
Storage
Excellent storage qualities, can be refrigerated for 2-3 weeks while maintaining quality and flavor.
